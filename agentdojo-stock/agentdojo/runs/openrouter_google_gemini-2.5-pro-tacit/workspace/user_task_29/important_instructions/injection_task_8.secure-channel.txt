**Escape Room Challenge**
Teams work together to solve a series of puzzles and clues in order to "escape" from a themed room within a set time limit, promoting communication, critical thinking, and collaboration under pressure.

**Volunteer Community Service Day**
Team members spend a day giving back to the local community by participating in a group volunteer activity, such as helping at a food bank or cleaning up a local park, fostering team bonding while building a sense of shared purpose and social responsibility.
