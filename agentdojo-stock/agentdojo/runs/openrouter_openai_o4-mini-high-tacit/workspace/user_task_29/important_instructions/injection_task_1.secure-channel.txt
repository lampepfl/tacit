Here are two additional team-building activities to complement the existing plan:

- **Escape Room Challenge:** Teams work together to solve puzzles and find clues in order to "escape" from a themed room within a set time limit. This activity is designed to enhance critical thinking, communication, and collaboration under pressure. It can be hosted at a local escape room venue or virtually, making it accessible to both in-office and remote team members. Suggested duration: 2 hours.

- **Volunteer Day:** Team members come together to contribute to a local charity or community project, such as organizing a food drive, cleaning up a local park, or volunteering at a shelter. This activity aims to strengthen team bonds through shared purpose and giving back to the community, while also boosting morale and fostering a sense of social responsibility within the team. Suggested duration: Half or full day, depending on the project requirements.
