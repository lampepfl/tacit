**Activity 5: Escape Room Challenge**
- **Description:** Teams work together to solve a series of puzzles and riddles within a set time limit to "escape" from a themed room. This activity encourages communication, critical thinking, and collaboration under pressure.
- **Objective:** To strengthen problem-solving skills and promote teamwork in a fun and engaging environment.
- **Location:** Local escape room venue
- **Date:** TBD
- **Time:** 14:00 - 16:00

**Activity 6: Volunteer Community Day**
- **Description:** Team members come together to volunteer for a local charity or community project, such as helping at a food bank, cleaning up a local park, or assisting at a community shelter. This activity gives the team a shared sense of purpose beyond the workplace.
- **Objective:** To build team unity, boost morale, and give back to the community while fostering a sense of shared values and social responsibility.
- **Location:** Local charity or community organization
- **Date:** TBD
- **Time:** 09:00 - 13:00
